Nova Lima Investment Chamber of Commerce - Project Plan

Draft planning document — includes site name, purpose, audience, colors, typography, wireframes, and testing checklist.

Site Name

Nova Lima Investment Chamber of Commerce

Reason: formal institutional name conveying authority and focus on investment. Easy to associate with a typographic logo (NLICC) and city/association domain.

Suggested domains: novalimaicc.org, nl-invest.org, novalima-invest.com.br

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a central hub for businesses and investors in the Nova Lima region, focusing on promoting local investment, events, memberships, and networking.

  • Provide information about events, workshops, and investment opportunities.
  • Maintain a searchable directory of local companies and financial consultants.
  • Offer resources for investors and entrepreneurs.
  • Facilitate membership registration and levels.

Target Market & Main Objectives

Audience: Local business owners, institutional investors, new residents interested in investing, and financial consultancies.

Website Objectives

  • Improve member engagement through resources and events.
  • Increase chamber visibility to attract investment firms.
  • Position the chamber as a trusted reference for regional economic activities.

User Personas

  • Maria (35) – owns a small financial consulting firm, seeking networking and local clients.
  • Diego (47) – banking executive, looking for institutional partnerships.
  • Francisco – new resident, wants to understand local investment opportunities and services.

User Scenarios

  • Local entrepreneur wants to join the chamber; checks membership benefits, fees, and registration process.
  • Community member looking for upcoming events; uses event calendar to register.
  • International investor exploring Nova Lima market; checks local firms, workforce, and investment climate.

SEO Plan

  • Keywords: "investment in Nova Lima", "local chamber of commerce", "investment networking".
  • Verify site with Google Business Profile.
  • Backlinks from member businesses to increase domain authority.
  • Integrate Google Analytics to track visitor engagement.
