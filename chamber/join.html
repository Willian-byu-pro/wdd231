Chamber Membership Join Page





Non profit Membership Level

Cost: $0 / year

Benefits:

Public listing in the partner directory.

Access to exclusive news and newsletters.

Priority in volunteer and local partnership calls.

Invitations to community events (with reduced fees when applicable).

Gold Membership Level

Cost: $1,200 / year

Benefits:

Featured position on the homepage (carousel or main banner).

Free booth at one annual organizational event.

VIP invitations and networking with sponsors and key leaders.

Advanced training and annual marketing consulting session.

Silver Membership Level

Cost: $420 / year

Benefits:

Three free workshops per year.

Advertisement in a monthly section (medium-sized banner on the site).

Highlighted position in the member directory by category.

Additional discounts through partnerships and premium events.

Bronze Membership Level

Cost: $120 / year

Benefits:

10% discount on events and training sessions.

Inclusion in the regional directory with a link to your organization’s website.

Invitations to monthly meetups and local events.

Access to a basic digital promotion kit.
